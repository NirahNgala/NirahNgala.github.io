ABOUT US

AFRIKAZI is an online marketplace that connects you with a network of pre-approved and background-checked individuals who have the time and skill sets needed to complete the listed tasks. It allows you to outsource small, repeat jobs and tasks ranging from housekeeping, delivery services, saloon & hairdressers,handyman services, mechanics, security, transportation to more professional services like accounting and legal represantation
Features and Facts
AFRIKAZI.COM is empowering the youth in kenya to ethically put their skills to work so as to become financially and professionally independent. We seeks to make a mark in the Kenyan on-demand goods and service provision space and in turn give our kenya youth a meaningful livelihood and a sense of purpose.
